Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL): An Excellent Turnaround Story

By
Fahad Saleem, Contributing Editor
-
05/29/2017
0
298
Share on Facebook
Tweet on Twitter
  • tweet

Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL) is an attractive stock with good prospects for investors in the near future. The Bermuda, Hamilton based company’s stock has gained over 27% year-to-date. The company posted excellent first quarter results recently. Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL) earned $0.24 per share in the quarter, better than the average analyst estimate of $0.21. Revenue in the quarter came in at $579 million, versus the consensus estimate of $570.67 million. In the second quarter of 2018, Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL) expects to earn $0.26-$0.30, versus the consensus of $0.25. Revenue in the same period is expected to come in at $585-$615 million, while the Street was expecting $591.67 million.

In the first quarter, Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL) divested its LTE modem business to ASR Microelectronics, generating proceeds of $45 million.

Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL) CEO Matt Murphy said in a statement during earnings call that the company is now focusing on its core business of storage, networking and connectivity. Mr. Murphy said that Marvell expects long-term secular growth in the future because the amount of data created and stored is burgeoning every day.

Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL) margins are also expanding. Investment firm Oppenheimer analyst Rick Schafer recently increased his rating for the stock to “Outperform” citing healthy margins and growth prospects. The analyst has a $23 price target on the stock. Rick thinks that Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL) is ahead of schedule on its “turnaround story”.

Several investment firms are bullish on Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL). Morgan Stanley recently increased its price target on the stock to $18.50. Deutsche Bank also bumped up its price target on the stock to $18 last week. Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL) competes with giants like Micron and Samsung in the lucrative solid-state drives industry.

Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL) has a strong balance sheet and no debt. Almost half of its revenue comes from its R&D arm. The company has a lot of intangible assets. As of the end of January 2015, Marvell has 5,300 U.S. patents and about 1,400 foreign patents with expiration dates ranging from 2015 to 2035.

Last year, activist hedge fund Starboard Value acquired a positon in Marvell Technology Group Ltd. (NASDAQ:MRVL).
