Ford Motor Company (F) Stock Will Benefit From Alexa Deal With Amazon.com, Inc.

Posted by Arie Goren on January 20, 2017, 06:17 AM EST
Related Tickers: AMZN , F

AMZN and F stocks will benefit from the integration of Amazon's Alexa in Ford cars.

  • The possibility to be able to control devices and services by voice commands will make Ford's vehicles more attractive.
  • Since other car makers would probably follow Ford's move, it will benefit Amazon as well.
  • In my view, both Amazon (NASDAQ:AMZN) and Ford (NYSE:F) stocks are a smart long-term investment.

Flickr

At the 2017 Consumer Electronics Show (CES) that took place in Las Vegas between January 5 to 8, Ford Motor Company (NYSE:F) announced a new collaboration with Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ:AMZN) which will enable consumers to use Alexa in Ford cars. Amazon Alexa has become very popular, and many people have received an Echo or Echo Dot this holiday as a gift. According to Ford, Alexa in its cars will provide access to shop, search, and control smart home features while driving. What's more, the integration of Alexa in Ford cars will allow Ford vehicle owners, by using voice commands, to remotely start the car from home, lock or unlock doors. Also, while driving, Ford car owners will be able to order items on Amazon, listen to audio books, and transfer local destinations to the in-car navigation system.

Don Butler, executive director, Ford Connected Vehicle and Services, explained:

“Ford and Amazon are aligned around a vision that your voice should be the primary way to interface with your favorite devices and services. Customers will be able to start their vehicles from home, and manage smart home features while on the road – making life easier.”

Steve Rabuchin, vice president, Amazon Alexa, said:

“We’re excited to work with Ford to enhance the driver experience both inside and outside of the vehicle. We believe voice is the future, and this is particularly true in cars. The ability to use your voice to control your smart home, access entertainment, manage to-do lists and more makes for an extraordinary driving experience. We can’t wait for Ford customers to try this out.”

As I see it, the integration of Alexa in Ford cars will benefit both companies, Ford and Amazon. The possibility of controlling devices and services by voice commands would make Ford's vehicles more attractive. Since other car makers probably would follow Ford's move, it will benefit Amazon as well.

What To Expect From Ford's Next Earnings Report

Ford Motors is scheduled to report its fourth quarter financial results on Thursday, January 26, before markets open. According to 18 analysts' average estimate, Ford is expected to post a profit of $0.36 a share, a 38% decline from its actual earnings for the same quarter a year ago. The highest estimate is for a profit of $0.42 a share while the lowest is for a profit of $0.32 a share. Revenue for the fourth quarter is expected to decrease 6.9% year over year to $35.22 billion, according to 15 analysts' average estimate. There were three up earnings per share revisions during the last 30 days. Since Ford has shown earnings per share surprise in four of its last seven quarters, as shown in the table below, there is a chance that the company will beat estimates also in the fourth quarter.

What To Expect From Amazon's Next Earnings Report

Amazon is scheduled to report its fourth quarter financial results on Thursday, January 26, after market close. According to 36 analysts' average estimate, Amazon is expected to post a profit of $1.36 a share, a 36% increase from its actual earnings for the same quarter a year ago. The highest estimate is for a profit of $1.86 a share while the lowest is for a profit of $0.36 a share, quite a big difference. Revenue for the fourth quarter is expected to increase 25% year over year to $44.67 billion, according to 35 analysts' average estimate. There were two earnings per share down revisions during the last 30 days.
