Amazon's Top-Selling Dash Products
Manikandan Raman , Benzinga Staff Writer  
November 25, 2016 12:41pm   Comments
Share:
Related AMZN
Discovery Acquires Scripps Network In $11.9 Billion Deal
Discovery Shares Plunge On Earnings, News Of $14.6 Billion Deal To Acquire Scripps Interactive
The Vetr community has upgraded $AMZN to 4.5-Stars. (Vetr)

The Amazon.com, Inc. (NASDAQ: AMZN) Dash Button is popular with consumers, as the Wi-Fi connected device reorders your favorite product with the press of a button.

Customers can buy a Dash Button for $4.99 and receive a credit for $4.99 after the first press. The Dash Button, which is available only for Prime members, is mainly used to purchase essentials.

Following are some of the best sellers in Amazon Dash Button:

  • All brand laundry detergent.
  • Amazon Basics batteries.
  • Angel Soft.
  • Arm & Hammer cat litter.
  • Bounty.
  • Cascade.
  • Charmin.
  • Cheez-It.
  • Clorox disinfecting pipes.
  • FIJI water.
  • Finish dishwasher detergent.
  • Milk-Bone.
  • Tide pods and powder.
  • Trojan.
  • Ziploc bags.

Meanwhile, the online retail giant has unleashed more than 75,000 deals for Cyber Monday, with new deals as often as every five minutes on toys, electronics and fashion items.

For Prime members, Amazon is offering 30-minute early access with shipping options, including 2-day shipping on more than 30 million items, Prime free same-day delivery on more than one million items available in 29 cities.

At last check, Amazon was trading flat on the day about 20 minutes before Friday's abbreviated session close.
