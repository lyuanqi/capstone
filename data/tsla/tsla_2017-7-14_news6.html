2 Stocks You Can’t Afford to Pass Up: Halliburton Company (HAL), Tesla, Inc. (TSLA)

Also, it has an estimated price-earnings (P/E) multiple of 17.14 and a trailing 12-month price-earnings (P/E) multiple of 0.
