4 Reasons to Consider Tesla Motors Inc’s (TSLA) Stock Now

April 11, 2016 By S.Mulhem Leave a Comment Filed Under: Automotive, News, Stocks

Tesla Motors Inc (NASDAQ:TSLA) is perhaps one of the most innovative firms based in the United States. Tesla, led by CEO Elon Musk, has become well known for designing, developing and manufacturing luxury electric vehicles, including the recently launched Model 3. The company is becoming an increasingly popular stock, making it highly liquid (like other popular stocks e.g. Amazon.com, Inc (NASDAQ:AMZN).)

On the morning of the 7th of April 2016, in a blog post, Tesla Motors Inc (NASDAQ:TSLA) revealed that 325,000 Model 3 vehicles had been reserved, in just one week after its launch. The launch of their model 3 was described as “the biggest product launch ever”, with an effective sales revenue of $14 billion so far.

Buying shares in Tesla has several advantages

1)Potential to Make Profit via Movement in Share Price

Many investors buy a stock with no interest of receiving a dividend payment, instead, they want the value of the stock to rise, and then sell it at a profit. Indeed, trading in this manner can have be highly profitable. Tesla Motors Inc’s (NASDAQ:TSLA) stock is likely to increase in value in the future, as the figures above suggests that their cars are in high demand, and there is no reason for this to change. Tesla Motors Inc (NASDAQ:TSLA) CEO Elon Musk has proven to be an excellent leader, for the firm and for other firms as well e.g. SolarCity Corp (NASDAQ:SCTY).

2)Tesla is An Ethical Stock

Certain traders choose to avoid trading stocks such as Lockheed Martin Corporation (NYSE:LMT), due to the disputed, unethical nature of manufacturing armaments. Tesla Motors Inc (NASDAQ:TSLA) benefits the environment, by bringing electric vehicles to the mass market, thus, reducing carbon emissions and allowing for better use of our limited crude oil reserves.

3)Potential for High Dividend Payments

Investing in Tesla Motors Inc (NASDAQ:TSLA) can also be used as part of a long term strategy. There is clearly a lot of demand for their products, and the brand is developing strong loyalty for customers. This is essentially a recipe for wide profit margins, as the firm has (to some extent) a monopoly over the electric car market, although Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (ETR:BMW) does have a presence in the market.

Furthermore, in light of the high levels of demand for the Model 3, the firm announced that they will be increasing their production capacity. This may allow them to take advantage of large scale of economies, leading to falling costs (per unit), and widening profit margins. This all supports the prediction of high dividend payments in the future.

4) High Levels of Liquidity

This essentially means that is is easy for you to buy or sell a share in the firm, due to the high levels of demand and supply for their stock. This gives investors the opportunity to easily take up a position, or to easily get cash in exchange for their shares.

Tesla Summary

Ultimately, Tesla Motors Inc (NASDAQ:TSLA) is a very attractive investment proposition. The firm began operating in 2003. They became publicly traded on the NASDAQ on the 29th June 2010, issuing 13.3 million shares to the public at an initial price of $17. A share in Tesla Motors Inc (NASDAQ:TSLA) was trading at $250.07 as the market closed on the 8th of April 2016 (an increase of 1371%).
