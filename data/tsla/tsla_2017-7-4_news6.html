Tesla Inc (TSLA) Model 3 Deliveries to Kickstart from July 28

Published By News Desk at July 4, 2017 08:13 am 30 reservation holders will be handed over their Model 3

For the last few weeks, we have been hearing a lot of things about Model 3 but the most talked about thing was the production and delivery timeline of the vehicle. Reservation holders, stakeholders, analysts, and everyone else only had these questions in mind as to when the company will start final production phase and when the delivery of the vehicles will begin.

Musk has now revealed the answers to both the questions. While production is slated to start this Friday, Musk in his tweet said that the deliveries of the vehicles would be done on Friday, July 28. According to a report by Teslarati, the Palo Alto Company will be holding a launch event or the handover party for the low-cost sedan on the last Friday of the current month.

During the handover event, 30 reservation holders will be handed over the keys while deliveries to other reservation holders will follow later. Tesla has already said that they will exponentially increase the production of Model 3. Hence one can expect that the deliveries of the sedan will also increase exponentially.

For now, Model 3 with an only single motor will be available followed by dual-motor Model 3 and finally the performance variant of the car in 2018. It is expected that the Model 3 launch event will not only feature deliveries, but it will also have a presentation about Model 3 features, performance, and battery range by Musk himself.

But for now, there are less than four weeks remaining till the final deliveries are made, and Tesla is yet to launch the online configurator of the car. The limited version of the Design Studio for Model 3 will allow reservation holders to choose the color of their choice along with the wheel type
