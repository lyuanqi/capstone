Advanced Micro Devices, Inc, Tesla Inc, Valeant Pharmaceuticals - Short Interest Update

Posted by Sreekanth Anasa on June 12, 2017, 06:02 AM EST
Related Tickers: AMD , TSLA , VRX

The latest short interest data is out for AMD, Tesla and Valeant. Have a look.

The latest short interest numbers are now out and probably, the likes of Sunnyvale, California-based Advanced Micro Devices Inc (NASDAQ:AMD), Palo Alto, California-based Tesla Inc (NASDAQ:TSLA) and Canada-based Valeant Pharmaceuticals Intl Inc (NYSE:VRX) could be among the most eagerly watched counters. The month of May has been a forgettable one for AMD investors with the stock losing nearly 18%. However, the semiconductor company's shares seem to be on a recovery path of late. Tesla stock continues to march northwards with its sight set on the $400 mark as Model 3 deadline approaches. Valeant stock had made some massive post-earnings gains but it has seen a significant correction since then. Now, let's take a closer look at the new short interest information for these companies.

AMD Short interest climbs further up.

Short interest in AMD stock has seen a further increase of 8.5% as per the latest available data. The short interest is as of May 31 which was released publicly a few days ago. In the last reporting period (May 15), the chipmaker had seen a massive 20.9% spike in short interest. The shares of the chip company have been very volatile during this reporting period with the number of shorted shares rising to 158.58M from 146.13M shares on May 15. The present short interest constitutes 19% of the total float with days to cover currently at 2 days. AMD stock short interest is at a one-year high.

Last week, AMD stock had its best run in recent times during which it gained more than 12%. The CPU and GPU maker has seen multiple positives emerging recently. The biggest of them being the resurgence of cryptocurrencies driving the demand for the company's GPUs. The stock's latest upward march has resulted in breakouts above its key 20-day, 50-day and 100-day Simple Moving Averages (SMA). However, the last trading session saw AMD shares closing below its long-term 100-day SMA. That said, the company has its flagship Vega GPU launch scheduled at the end of this month. Investors should closely watch out for the Vega launch, as this could be the next major catalyst for AMD.

Short interest in Tesla stock cools off a bit.

By the looks of it, some shorts seem to have saved themselves from more pain. Short interest in TSLA stock fell by 2.3%. Short interest declined from 31.69 million shares during the preceding reporting period, to 30.96 million shares as of May-end. As a percentage of float, short interest is still very high but declined marginally from 27.2% to 26.6%. Further, the days to cover rose from 5 to 6.

The Elon Musk-led company is soon approaching its Model 3 delivery July deadline. With Tesla's announcement to meet the July deadline for Model 3 production, investors are very upbeat. The corporate governance concerns do not seem to bother Tesla shareholders much. The Model 3 launch could be the inflection point for Tesla stock. The shares of the automaker are expected to continue rallying going into the new car launch. However, an unfavorable result with respect to the July launch could hurt the stock's fortunes in a big way.

Short interest in Valeant stock sees a dip.

Between the two reporting dates of short interest numbers( May 15 & May 31), Valeant stock has seen a significant correction losing nearly 15%. During the same period, the short interest in Valeant Pharmaceuticals declined by 6.8%. The number of shorted shares on the latest reporting date decreased to 50.47 million from 54.17 million shares at the end of the preceding fortnight. As a percentage of float, short interest dropped to 14.9%, from 16%. The days to cover came in at three days.

Shares of the pharma company continue to struggle due to its past issues. The heavy debt burden has been an issue for the company for some time now but things are beginning to change. The rumor of sale of assets is the new factor which is driving the stock of late. However, VRX stock still has a long way to go. Further, the stock is facing strong resistance from its short-term 20-day SMA.
